/* About */

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. Previously, I earned my B.S. in Chemistry from the Zhiyuan College (Zhiyuan Honors Program) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

/* Research Experience */

During my undergraduate studies, I worked with Prof. Xiaodong Zhuang at SJTU, and Prof. Joseph T. Hupp in the Department of Chemistry at Northwestern University.

/* Research Interests */

My current research interests focus on AI4Science, particularly the integration of Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations with Machine Learning.
